General Rules

  1. Cells have a living condition based on the number of neighbors. If there are too little or too many neighbors, the cell will die.
  2. All other live cells die in the next generation. All other dead cells stay dead.
  3. The user will determine the living conditions via number of neighbors for cells to stay alive, spawn, or die.

About the Game of Life and our version
